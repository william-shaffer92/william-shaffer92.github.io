Frequently Asked Questions

Is StayGreen detectable by my employer?

StayGreen simulates a standard Bluetooth Keyboard/Mouse. It does not install any software on your computer, making it appear as a normal hardware device. The pro features add layers of human-like randomness to make the activity indistinguishable from manual use.Disclaimer: While we've designed StayGreen to be as undetectable as possible, users should be aware of their company's specific policies and use the app at their own risk.

Will this drain my phone's battery?

No. The app is designed to be highly energy-efficient. It runs as a lightweight background service with a persistent notification, using minimal battery to maintain the Bluetooth connection and send input signals.

Do I need to keep the app open?

Once you start the activity, you can let the app run in the background. A notification will show that the service is active, and it will continue to work even if your phone screen is off.

What are text profiles for?

Text profiles allow you to specify what text the app should type. You can add code snippets, notes, or any text you want. The app will randomly select from your saved profiles for its typing activity. Pro users can create unlimited profiles!
